# Miro Board: Drivers & Action Items by Role

**Source:** Miro Board Screenshot - [Peer Loop] Project Board
**Date Captured:** 2025-11-30
**Section:** Drivers and Action Items for Student, Teacher Student, Course Creator roles

---

## DRIVERS

*"What are user needs? What can encourage users to engage to the platform?"*

### STUDENT Drivers

1. Friendly community of people that share the same interests
2. High-quality mentorship and synchronous communication with Teacher Students
3. Ability to pay for the courses (recoup a majority of cost)
4. AI Assistant to ease the learning process
5. Interesting and diverse content
6. Ability to find related courses easily
7. Ability to select a Teacher that matches my needs (random or student choice)
8. A way to request a content I'm searching for if it's not available
9. Ability to opt out at any point if Student is not satisfied with the Teacher Student
10. A separate certificate to confirm mastery (different from the certificate of completion) (Driver #10)

### TEACHER STUDENT Drivers

1. Ability to participate in the community and access the algorithmic feed
2. Ability to recover the training costs and obtain a potential source of income
3. An efficient synchronous method to communicate with Students
4. A clear and convenient process to apply for Teacher Students and prove own skills
5. Ability to opt out at any point if Teacher is not satisfied with a Student
6. Achievement of verifiable mastery - Return on investment (career advancement)

### COURSE CREATOR (Author) Drivers

1. A user-friendly course management system
2. Ability to build and lead a strong community
3. Ability to receive royalty for their courses
4. A clear and convenient process of approving new Teacher Students
5. Ability to monitor student activity on their courses
6. Ability to monitor the quality of teaching with their materials
7. Build a loyal tribe with high switching cost

---

## ACTION ITEMS

*Platform features and functionality needed to address the drivers*

### STUDENT Action Items

- A community tool for discussions, events, news and updates (Bluesky) (Driver #1)
- A tool for synchronous communication with the Teacher Student (Driver #2)
- Payment system (use case: pay for the courses, with the ability to get a refund if they don't like it) (Driver #3)
- AI Assistant (Driver #4)
- Newsletters (Driver #6)
- A separate certificate to confirm mastery (different from the certificate of completion) (Driver #10)

### TEACHER STUDENT Action Items

- A community tool for discussions, events, news and updates (Bluesky) (Driver #1)
- Payment system (use case: obtain commission for each new mentored student) (Driver #2)
- A tool for synchronous communication with Students (Driver #3 and 5)
- Functionality to apply for Teacher Student (Driver #4)
- A points system to motivate Teacher - Teacher becomes more active
- Functionality to review and select Teacher Student (e.g., select by schedule) (Driver #7)
- A form to request content if it's not available (Driver #8)

### COURSE CREATOR (Author) Action Items

- A platform for content creation and management (Driver #1)
- A community tool for discussions, events, news and updates (Bluesky) (Driver #2)
- Payment system (use case: earn royalty for each new student) (Driver #3)
- Functionality to approve Teacher Students (Driver #4)
- Extended course analytics (Driver #5)
- Access to student feedback on each Teacher Student on the course (Driver #6)
- Recommendations / autosuggested content (Driver #5)
- Flexible filtering (Driver #6)
- Usage based fee (per session) to creator for unlimited use of Big Blue Button

---

## Key Observations

1. **Bluesky Mentioned:** Community tool explicitly references Bluesky for discussions, events, news, updates (appears in all three roles)
2. **Certificate Types:** Two distinct certificates identified:
   - Certificate of completion (standard)
   - Certificate of mastery (separate, for proving deeper understanding)
3. **Opt-Out Ability:** Both Students AND Teacher Students need ability to exit relationships
4. **Points/Gamification:** Points system mentioned to motivate Teacher Students
5. **BBB Usage Fee:** Creator pays usage-based fee for Big Blue Button (per session)
6. **Content Requests:** Students can request content that doesn't exist yet
7. **Schedule-Based Selection:** Teacher Student selection can be based on schedule availability
8. **Career Advancement:** Teacher Students see ROI as career advancement, not just income
