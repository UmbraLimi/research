# AI Tools Overview - Course Overview

## Course Description

Navigate the overwhelming world of AI tools with confidence. This practical course cuts through the noise and helps you understand what AI tools exist, what they're actually good for, and how to choose the right ones for your specific needs - without getting lost in the hype.

The AI landscape is exploding with new tools every week. ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, Cursor, DALL-E, Gemini - the list goes on. Instead of drowning you in more information, this course gives you a framework for understanding and evaluating AI tools. You'll learn to think strategically about AI, make smart tool choices, and build your own toolkit without the overwhelm.

Perfect for complete beginners, curious professionals, and anyone tired of FOMO from seeing too many AI tools they don't understand. You'll get hands-on experience with representative tools from each major category, learn decision-making frameworks, and leave with a clear plan for using AI in your work and life.

## What You'll Learn

### Session 1: Text & Code AI
- **Understanding AI Categories** - Major types of AI tools, how they differ, reducing overwhelm with clear frameworks
- **AI Writing Tools** - ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini - what each does best, when to use which, hands-on practice
- **AI Coding Tools** - Claude Code, Cursor, GitHub Copilot - capabilities, differences, choosing for your skill level
- **Decision Framework** - How to evaluate and choose AI tools for specific tasks without getting overwhelmed

### Session 2: Visual AI & Your Personal Toolkit
- **AI Image Generation** - Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion - what's possible, use cases, hands-on creation
- **AI Video Generation** - Runway, Pika, Sora - current capabilities, when to use, limitations
- **Other AI Tools Landscape** - Productivity tools (Notion AI, Otter.ai), research tools (Perplexity), voice AI
- **Building Your AI Toolkit** - Match tools to your actual needs, avoid shiny object syndrome
- **Staying Current Without Overwhelm** - Framework for evaluating new AI tools strategically

## Why This Course?

**Reduce Overwhelm** - Learn a framework for understanding AI tools instead of trying to learn every tool that exists.

**Make Smart Choices** - Develop decision-making skills to choose the right tool for each task, saving time and money.
