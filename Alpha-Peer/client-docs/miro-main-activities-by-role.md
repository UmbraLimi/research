# Miro Board: Main Activities by Role

**Source:** Miro Board Screenshot - [Peer Loop] Project Board
**Date Captured:** 2025-11-30
**Section:** Main Activities by User Role

---

## STUDENT

### Primary Activities (Blue cards)
- Sign up / sign in
- Browse courses
- Pay and enroll to courses
- Complete courses
- Apply for Teacher Student

### Secondary Activities (Light blue cards)
- Participate in discussions, Ongoing engagement, earning goodwill points, become a power user
- Select a Teacher Student - Matchmaking (Random Teacher is the default option)

### Pain Points / Challenges (Pink cards)
- Lack of human connection and the feel of isolation that leads to high dropout rates
- Lack of feedback and synchronous communication
- Complicated and confusing learning flow (I don't have a way to ask if I don't understand something)
- Irrelevant content
- Inability to matchmake a Teacher suitable for my needs

---

## TEACHER STUDENT

### Primary Activities (Blue cards)
- Apply to be a teacher, Creator approves all applications
- Some type of introduction to teach, Rules during application process
- Track student activity
- Obtain commission for each mentored student
- Provide feedback to students, collect and relay feedback to creator
- Create calendar of availability
- Earn teaching certificate

### Pain Points / Challenges (Pink cards)
- The need to subsidize the training and recover the costs
- The need to provide timely feedback to students
- Confusing and inconsistent process to apply for Teacher Student
- The risk that student doesn't like Teacher Student and wants to bail out (and vice versa)
- Amplify creator content

---

## COURSE CREATOR (AUTHOR)

### Primary Activities (Blue cards)
- Develop and manage course curriculum
- Assign Teacher Students
- Earn royalty of the course fee
- Gather feedback via polls, content difficulty to refine course over time
- Host AMA sessions, encourage students to ask questions, build excitement around launch
- Build personal branding, foster loyalty
- Appoint moderator assistants, monitor community health, post announcements, create challenges

### Secondary Activities (Light blue cards)
- Share student success stories to attract new students
- Possibly use studio to publish newsletter? Can we do monthly subscription payments for newsletter?

### Pain Points / Challenges (Pink cards)
- The need to build a strong and supportive community
- Make sure Teacher Students are skilled enough and can provide high-quality mentorship
- Be sure that their course will attract students don't get lost in the general pool
- Lack of content
- The need for unlimited real time communications via big blue button for creators

## PLATFORM ADMIN

### Primary Activities (Blue cards)
- Manage platform configurations
- User management
- Access and monitor platform analytics

## COMMUNITY MODERATOR

*Note: This appears to be a NEW ROLE not previously documented*

### Primary Activities (Blue cards)
- Support staff
- Answer questions
- Troubleshoot issues
- Moderate course-related chats
- Add users to closed chat

---

## Key Observations

1. **New Role Identified:** Community Moderator is a distinct role separate from Course Creator
2. **Teacher Student Certification:** There's a path to "Earn teaching certificate"
3. **Matchmaking System:** Students can select Teacher Students, with random matching as default
4. **Commission Model:** Teacher Students earn commission per mentored student
5. **Creator Revenue:** Course Creators earn royalty of course fees
6. **Newsletter Feature:** Question raised about studio for newsletters with subscription payments
7. **Pain Points Pattern:** Many pain points relate to communication, feedback, and community building
